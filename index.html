About Us

The roleplayers ready for adventure!

We started out as six friends sitting around a dining room table, all learning to play D&D together. In our early days we got a lot of rules wrong, but we had fun doing it. It's given us the strong ethos of "Better it be thematically correct than lawfully correct" during our games.

After several years, and four campaigns, we went our seperate ways towards higher education. We kept in touch and started a new campaign online. We even found local D&D groups within our Universities. Then comes after University, we all came home with our respective degrees and more tales of heroism and guile. Now, we play in person again on weekends, and have even expanded that into this weekend club. With the advent of streaming platforms, and games such as High Rollers and Critical Role, D&D has certainly seen a resurgence in popularity.

So here we are, with our weekend club, teaching anyone who wants to learn how to play. Recently we have expanded beyond purely Dungeons & Dragons, to include Call of Cthulu games. But whether you're here for a one-shot or a full campaign, everyone is welcome to join the Swordcoast Expeditionary Guild!

Learners club

Here at the Learners Club we have seasoned adventurers who can help you with the basic rules of gameplay, most important of all: have fun!

You'll even get to create a character and play a beginner friendly one-shot (that's a game that only lasts one session), so you can gain a better understanding of the rules. Don't worry if you don't have dice, we have plenty lying around just waiting to be rolled.
